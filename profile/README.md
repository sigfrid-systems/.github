# Sigfrid Systems

**AI compliance infrastructure for Swedish public sector**

We help Swedish municipalities deploy AI safely with GDPR and EU AI Act compliance built in.

## What We Do

- **Shadow AI Mapping** — Discover unauthorized AI usage across your organization
- **Automated Documentation** — GDPR Article 30 processing records, auto-generated
- **EU AI Act Compliance** — Article 50 transparency markers for AI-generated content
- **Swedish Data Residency** — Azure Sweden and AWS Stockholm supported
